Security-first, not hype-first.

EnteleWALLET is planned around real user protection: clear transaction visibility, non-custodial control, safe recovery education, device-level warnings, and future integration with EnteleCLOS cybersecurity modules.

✓

Non-custodial principle

Users should remain in control of their own private keys, seed phrases, and wallet access credentials.

✓

Human-readable risk warnings

Transactions, signatures, unknown contracts, suspicious links, and risky approvals should be explained in simple language.

✓

Recovery education

Clear guidance for backups, seed phrase safety, device protection, and emergency recovery preparation.

✓

Compliance-ready access layers

Future support for verified user flows, business wallets, project dashboards, and regulated access environments.
