Security-first wallet access for the EnteleKRON ecosystem

EnteleWALLET is being built with a security-first approach for wallet verification, ecosystem asset monitoring, and future non-custodial wallet functionality.

1. Official EnteleWALLET Domains

entelewallet.com
app.entelewallet.com
entelewallet.app
entelewallet.org
wallet.entelekron.io
Always check the domain before connecting a wallet or signing any message.

2. EnteleWALLET Lite Safety

  • EnteleWALLET Lite does not store seed phrases.
  • EnteleWALLET Lite does not store private keys.
  • EnteleWALLET Lite does not custody funds.
  • EnteleWALLET Lite connects to existing wallets only.
  • Wallet verification uses safe message signatures.

3. Signature Safety

A valid EnteleWALLET signature must only verify wallet ownership.

  • It must not transfer tokens.
  • It must not approve spending.
  • It must not create a blockchain transaction.
  • It must not cost gas.
Never sign messages you do not understand.

4. Seed Phrase and Private Key Warning

EnteleWALLET, EnteleKRON, TVK Group, TVK Labs, SOVRA, support staff, admins, partners, or community moderators will never ask for:

  • seed phrase
  • private key
  • recovery phrase
  • wallet password
  • remote access to your computer
  • payment through social media messages

7. Phishing Protection

  • Never trust payment addresses sent by Telegram, Discord, X, Instagram, or email.
  • Always verify payment instructions inside the official investor dashboard.
  • Never install fake wallet extensions.
  • Bookmark the official domains.
  • Verify SSL/HTTPS.
  • Avoid sponsored search-result links for wallet access.
