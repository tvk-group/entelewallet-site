Section 02 · Lite Safety Model

EnteleWALLET Lite Safety

EnteleWALLET Lite is a wallet-connection and verification interface. It is intentionally minimal so that your keys and funds remain under your existing wallet's control.

✓
Does not store seed phrasesSeed phrases are never entered, transmitted or stored in EnteleWALLET Lite.
✓
Does not store private keysPrivate keys always stay inside your own wallet — MetaMask, Rabby, Ledger and other supported wallets.
✓
Does not custody fundsEnteleWALLET Lite never holds, moves or transfers assets. It has no custody role.
✓
Connects to existing wallets onlyEnteleWALLET Lite works as a read-and-verify layer on top of the wallets you already use.
✓
Wallet verification uses safe message signaturesOwnership is confirmed through off-chain message signatures that never move tokens or trigger a transaction.
Section 03 · Signature Safety

Signature Safety

A valid EnteleWALLET signature must only verify wallet ownership. It must never behave like a transaction.

✍️

A valid EnteleWALLET signature must:

✓
Only verify wallet ownership.
⛔

A valid EnteleWALLET signature must NOT:

✕
Transfer tokens.
✕
Approve spending of any token.
✕
Create a blockchain transaction.
✕
Cost gas.
!
Never sign messages you do not understand. If a signature request asks for token approvals, transfers or on-chain actions, reject it and report it to our security team.
Section 04 · Critical Warning

Seed Phrase and Private Key Warning

The following list represents an absolute rule. Nobody from any of these organizations, roles or communities will ever ask for it.

! EnteleWALLET, EnteleKRON, TVK Group, TVK Labs, SOVRA, support staff, admins, partners or community moderators will NEVER ask for:

✕
Your seed phrase.
✕
Your private key.
✕
Your recovery phrase.
✕
Your wallet password.
✕
Remote access to your computer.
✕
Payment through Telegram, Discord, X, Instagram or any social media messages.

Anyone requesting any of the items above is attempting a scam. Do not respond. Report immediately.

Section 07 · Phishing Protection

Phishing Protection

Applied consistently, these rules eliminate the most common attack vectors used against Web3 users today.

1
Never trust payment addresses received via social channelsTelegram, Discord, X, Instagram and email are not trusted channels for wallet addresses or payment instructions.
2
Verify payment instructions inside the official investor dashboardOnly the official investor dashboard on a verified domain should be used to confirm any payment address.
3
Never install fake wallet extensionsOnly install browser extensions and mobile apps from the wallet's own official website or verified app stores.
4
Bookmark the official domainsBookmarking prevents typos and blocks lookalike domains from being opened by mistake.
5
Verify SSL/HTTPSConfirm the padlock and HTTPS certificate belong to entelewallet.com or a listed official domain before signing anything.
6
Avoid sponsored search-result linksAttackers regularly buy sponsored ads that impersonate wallet websites. Type the domain manually or use your bookmark.
